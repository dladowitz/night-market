Ready to Start?

Village Market is an online food planning tool. It was born out of organizing food for dozens of Startup Weekend events. It's hard planning seven meals over three days. There are hundreds of things than need to happen to make an event successful. We are here to help. We've been there.

How it works?

Events and Meals

List out your events and meals to keep track of what you're serving. Track food preferences and head count.

Budgets and Costs

Keep track of how much you are spending. We'll warn you if you are over budget. See meal and person costs.

Scheduling

Timing is everything when it comes to food. Keep track of delivery and pickup times.

Recommendations

We've designed this app to show you what works and warn you about common pitfalls.

Supplies

Don't forget plates, fork and napkins. Keep track of all the supplies you'll need for the event.

Vendors

Access a growing database of food vendors used by other events in your area (coming soon).
